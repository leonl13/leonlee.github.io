About Me

All the things that make me, me.

Experienced in Adaptability

I strive to do what the role requires of me and to fit into the team seamlessly. I've worked in both start-ups and MNCs, in various industries (e-commerce, construction, finance), in different countries around the world.

Self-taught Lifelong Learner

Whether it's work related or simply a new hobby, my passion lies in learning new things (and I'd like to think that I'm fast at it.) I've received certificates in coding & data analysis, including the Google Data Analytics Certificate. Side note, Google is my best friend.

Tech Savvy

I pride myself in being able to use Excel without a mouse (and having a 130WPM average). In terms of coding, I prefer Python over R, and SQL for querying. Fun fact: I even made my first computer game on BYOND when I was 12.

Finance Educated, Market Trained

Analytical, Strategic, Rational Mindset

Driven by Passion
